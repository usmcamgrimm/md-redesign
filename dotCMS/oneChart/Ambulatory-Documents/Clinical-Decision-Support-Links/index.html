Clinical Decision Support Links

Adult CDS Interventions

  • BMI Screening and Follow Up - NQF 0421

    • Automatically add a problem to the patient’s problem list if the patient’s BMI falls above 30 (Obesity) or above 40 (Morbid Obesity)

  • Tobacco Use: Screening and Cessation Intervention - NQF 0028

    • When the Social History control is updated with an Active Smoking Status, place the Problem of Smoker on the patients chart.

  • Documentation of Current Medications in the Medical record - NQF 0419

    • Providers should complete med rec in the Depart module. If Med Rec has not been completed, the Med Rec section of Depart is highlighted yellow to alert the provider to complete Med Rec.

Pediatric CDS Interventions

  • Documentation of Current Medications in the Medical record - NQF 0419

    • Providers should complete med rec in the Depart module. If Med Rec has not been completed, the Med Rec section of Depart is highlighted yellow to alert the provider to complete Med Rec.

  • Weight Assessment and Counseling for Nutrition and Physical Activity for Children and Adolescents - NQF 0024

    • Automatically add a Overweight to the patient’s problem list if the patient’s BMI Percentile is between 85 and 95 for patients 2-18 years old and Obesity if BMI Percentile is greater than 95.

  • Tobacco Use: Screening and Cessation Intervention - NQF 0028

    • When the Social History control is updated with an Active Smoking status, place the Problem of Smoker on the patient's chart.
