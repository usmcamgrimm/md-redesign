Delineation of Privileges

Clinical privilege delineation is essential in assuring quality care. Practitioners may request privileges at initial appointment, reappointment, or during the interim for privileges not previously granted.

Use the grid below to find, review, and request privileges. Each privilege describes minimum requirements for fulfillment as well as documentation requirements. Verification of education or training will also be required.
