Allied Health Caregivers

Allied Health Caregivers participate in patient care under the supervision of a Medical Staff member.

AH Caregivers submit to a screening and authorization process unlike AH Professionals who apply via the Credentialing process. Methodist Healthcare Human Resources manages the authorization process for AH Caregivers.

All AH Caregiver applicants must use a valid personal email address as the primary source of communication. Methodist email addresses, e.g. @mlh.org or @lebonheur.org, are unacceptable.
